Privacy Policy for ThyTalk

Last Updated: February 11, 2023

At ThyTalk, we are committed to protecting your privacy and personal information.

This Privacy Policy outlines our practices for collecting, using, and sharing personal information when you use our social media platform at https://www.thytalk.com.

Information We Collect

We collect personal information from you when you use our platform, including:

  • Personal Information: We collect information that identifies you as an individual, such as your name, email address, and password when you create an account on our platform.
  • User Content: We collect the content you upload, post, or otherwise make available through the platform, such as messages, photos, and videos.
  • Device Information: We collect information about the devices you use to access the platform, such as your device type, operating system, and IP address.
  • Log Information: We log information about your use of the platform, such as the date and time you access the platform, the pages you visit, and the amount of time you spend on the platform.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve the platform: We use your information to operate and improve the platform and to provide you with the services and features we offer.
  • To communicate with you: We use your information to communicate with you, such as to respond to your support requests, to send you updates and news about the platform, and to send you promotional offers and advertisements.
  • To enforce our policies: We use your information to enforce our policies and to protect the rights, property, and safety of ThyTalk, our users, and others.

Sharing Your Information

We WILL NOT SHARE YOUR INFORMATION with third parties, unless:

  • With third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf, such as hosting, data storage, and email delivery.
  • For Legal Reasons: We may disclose your information if we believe that it is necessary to comply with a legal request, such as a subpoena or court order, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of ThyTalk, our users, or others.
  • In Connection with a Merger or Acquisition: If we are acquired by or merged with another company, we may share your information with the new owners of the company.

Your Choices

You have the following choices regarding your information:

  • Accessing and Correcting Your Information: You may access and correct your personal information by logging into your account and updating your profile.
  • Deactivating Your Account: You may deactivate your account by contacting us at support@thytalk.com. When you deactivate your account, we will remove your personal information from our platform, but some information may remain in our archives for legal or administrative purposes.

    • Data Security

    We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no security measures are perfect, and we cannot guarantee the security of your information.

    Changes to this Privacy Policy

    We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes to the policy, we will post the updated policy on this page and update the "Last Updated" date above. Your continued use of the platform after the updated policy becomes effective indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.
